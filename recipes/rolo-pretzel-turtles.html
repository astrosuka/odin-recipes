Rolo Pretzel Turtles

Description

This Rolo pretzel recipe is easy and fun to make with pretzels, caramel-covered chocolate candies, and pecan nuts for a delicious sweet treat. Store cooled turtles in an airtight container.

These pretzel turtles (a.k.a. pretzel Rolo pecan cookies) are a little bit sweet, a little bit salty, and 100% delicious.

Pretzel Turtle Ingredients

The three ingredients needed for this pretzel Rolo pecan cookie recipe aren't surprising

  • Mini pretzels
  • Chocolate-covered caramel candies (such as Rolos)
  • Pecan halves

How to Make Pretzel Rolo Pecan Cookies

It couldn't be easier to make these crowd-pleasing treats. You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below â€” but here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make these top-rated pretzel Rolo pecan cookies:

  1. Arrange the pretzels on a baking sheet.
  2. Place a candy on each pretzel.
  3. Bake until the chocolate softens.
  4. Press a pecan half into each warm candy.
  5. Cool completely.
